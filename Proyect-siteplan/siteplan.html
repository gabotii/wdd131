Site Plan for 3D Garden Planters

Site Name

3D Garden Planters

This name reflects the focus on innovative, three-dimensional gardening solutions that enhance plant growth and aesthetics.

Optional domain availability: 3dgardenplanters.com

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a resource for customers interested in purchasing and learning about 3D planters. It will provide product details, usage tips, and a blog with gardening advice.

Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of using 3D planters compared to traditional ones?
  • How do I care for my 3D planter?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #004C9E (Used for headings and buttons)

Accent Color: #00366B (Used for backgrounds and highlights)

Typography

Font 1: Arial, sans-serif (Used for body text)

Font 2: Georgia, serif (Used for headings)

Wireframe

Mobile View: Header with logo, navigation links, hero image, content section, footer.

Desktop View: Similar layout with a sidebar for recent articles.
